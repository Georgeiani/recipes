Penne All Vodka

Ingreidents needed

  • Pasta
  • Chopped shallots
  • Chopped garlic cloves
  • Chopped red chili
  • Tamato Puree
  • Vodka
  • Mascarpone
  • Pasta water
  • Salt and pepper

Recipe

  1. Cook pasta in plenty of lightly salted water. Keep 1 dl of the pasta water when you pour it off. Drizzle a little olive oil over the pasta.
  2. Finely chop onion, garlic and chilli. Place in a large frying pan and fry until soft in olive oil .
  3. Raise the temperature in the pan and add the tomato puree and let fry for a minute. Be careful not to burn it.
  4. Pour in vodka and let it simmer together with the tomato sauce for a few minutes.
  5. Stir in mascarpone and pasta water. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Stir the pasta into the sauce and serve with grated Parmigiano Reggiano, shredded basil and possibly crispy salsiccia.
